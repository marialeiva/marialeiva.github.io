Pain Points Unoptimized mobile assets causing slow load times. Loss of cart data when switching between devices. Repetitive manual typing on small touchscreens. Unexpected shipping fees appearing at the final step. Unclear delivery windows and cluttered confirmation.

Strategic Opportunities

Mobile-First UI

Ensure touch targets exceed 44px and forms utilize native mobile input types.

Session Integrity

Persistent cart states that sync across desktop and mobile to prevent drop-off.

Trust Building

Strategic placement of security badges and encryption notices at the payment step.

Clarity & Speed

Eliminate clutter in the final summary to highlight arrival dates and total costs.
