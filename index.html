Applying a Define–Measure–Analyze–Improve–Control mindset, I structured raw user feedback into a quantified problem statement, identified root-cause friction points across the 5-stage flow, and validated solutions through iterative prototyping — from lo-fi wireframe to tested hi-fi prototype, with no assumption left unverified.
